Network Participants Ecosystem

The $GURU powers ecosystem's Atomic Franchise Mechanism between contributors and users.
Gurus:
AI Processors, Compute Node Runners, and Individual Agents code contributors.
Ecosystem Projects:
Projects and products combining multiple BBPA orchestrations and data scenarios to solve specific tasks. These projects can publish their AI Processors, GURU AI Assistants, and Individual Workers in an infrastructure-as-a-service catalog.
Individual Agents:
AI/Non-Custodial Web3 Processors run by Compute nodes and used in BBPM-defined processes.
Integrators:
Middle layer providing B2B adoption of the Guru AI Flow Orchestrator and Framework.
Retail Users:
Utilize the network as Web3 automations engine and AI assistants from their wallets and participate in "Compute by Earn" functionality when idle.
